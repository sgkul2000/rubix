Reverse Code

An online coding contest with a twist! Powered by GeeksforGeeks and Scrimba, with the usual instructions of the question not available to the challenger, only the input and the outputs are provided, it is their duty to come up with the right logic and justify the provided outputs. Reverse Coding can be taken in any language of the participant's choice.

Join us as we hear from an eminent panel consisting of women stalwarts who have made great strides in their respective fields, and get to know their wonderful insights on the opportunities, challenges, and various other issues that women who wish to make a career for themselves in technology face in day-to-day life.
